Welcome to The Solar System website!

The purpose of this website is to provide all visitors with a basic understanding of our Solar System.

This website contains facts about the orbital speeds of our planets, the size of the Sun and our planets, and the distance between each planet!

At this scale, Earth takes roughly 3.65 seconds to completely orbit the Sun, and Neptune roughly 10 minutes!

In actuality, it takes Earth one year to complete an orbital period, and Neptune takes around 165 years!

NOTE: Orbits are not actually circular, they're elliptical; circles are used for orbital speed demonstration purposes.

At this scale, one pixel is roughly 500 miles! The Sun can barely even fit onto the screen, and Mercury is only 3px in width and height - barely visible.

NOTE: The distances between the planets and Sun is not correct, this demonstration is just to show the size of the planets and Sun.
